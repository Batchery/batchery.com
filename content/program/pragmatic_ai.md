We meet a lot of startup founders that are confused by AI. There is a lot of buzz out there, constant jargon, and it’s tough to figure out how it all applies to your great startup idea.

The Batchery Pragmatic AI program was designed to break through the hype and enable you with actionable knowledge for expanding your strategic vision with AI.

### Program Schedule

- **Tuesdays** - Interactive educational session
- **Thursdays** - Peer group idea generation sessions
- **On-Demand** - One-on-one mentoring with AI Experts

### Week-by-Week Breakdown

- **Week One** – Introduction
- **Week Two** – Language Models
- **Week Three** – Machine Learning
- **Week Four** - Responsible AI with Olivia Gambelin
- **Week Five** – Multimedia Applications
- **Week Six** – Agents & Automation
- **Week Seven** – New Revenue Streams with Your Data
